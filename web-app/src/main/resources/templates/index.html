Road dyno For car enthusiasts like you!

Welcome to road dyno!

A web app that can turn your data logs into real dyno graphs!

If you have a data logger, road dyno will let you to truly evaluate your mods!

... load your WOT log now!

Key features:

  • Real engine dynamometer, torque/hp plots for the entire RPM band
  • No estimates like 1/4 mi or 0-60 HP calculators, road dyno uses real measurements
  • Easily dyno any vehicle including 2WD and AWD vehicles
  • Automatically finds multiple runs in a single data log
  • Compare multiple graphs and save your plots to multiple formats
